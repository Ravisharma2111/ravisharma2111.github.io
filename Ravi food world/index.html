Healthy Idli

idli is one of South India's most popular dishes that is consumed and embraced in India and all around the world. It is generally consumed as breakfast. The main ingredients in idli include rice and black lentils. The method of preparation involves fermentation of the ingredients and then steaming the batter prepared from the same. There are variations available depending on the ingredients used. Some of them include tatte idli, rava idli, and ragi idli. Idli is often served along with sambar and varieties of chutney.
